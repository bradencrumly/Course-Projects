About Us

Inspire is a Paul Mitchell Focus salon located in Norman, Oklahoma. The salon offers various services such as, Custom Colors, Procession Haircutting, Bridal Events, Styling, B3 Treatments, Brazilian Blowout, Makeup, Waxing, Lashes and a variety of Aesthetics services. Inspire provides top of the line take home products with brands such as, Paul Mitchell, Leaf + Flower and BrazilIan Blowout to insure every guest receives the same results every single day.

Mission Statement

At Inspire PM Salon, we believe the experience is everything. We want our guests to feel pampered and relaxed during their visit and leave feeling beautiful and empowered. Our mission is to Transform hearts, one head at time. Not only do we strive to produce excellent results, we also want our guests feeling inspired to be the best versions of themselves by showing them love, strength and compassion. Continued education is essential to our salon family. It is very important that we provide our team with the freshest styles and techniques within the industry.

Vision Statement

Inspire Paul Mitchell Salon in Norman wishes to house a positive, fun-loving salon home for talented, aspiring salon professionals. We want our salon to provide an environment where they have the ability to learn, create and grow. Providing quality hair color & cutting in Norman,Oklahoma.
